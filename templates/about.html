What is this system?

This Breast Cancer Prediction System is built using a machine learning model trained on the Wisconsin Breast Cancer Dataset.

It analyzes medical metrics such as tumor size, texture, and symmetry to classify whether the cancer is likely to be Malignant (M) or Benign (B).

Why it matters:

  • Early detection increases treatment success rates
  • Helps provide direction for medical consultation
  • Empowers individuals with awareness

This tool is designed for educational and preliminary diagnostic purposes only. For medical advice, please consult a healthcare professional.
